Empowering the next generation of healthcare leaders in Zimbabwe through education, community service, and advocacy.

Why Join ZIMSA?

ZIMSA is a platform for medical students to grow professionally and make a difference in their communities. Through training, community health projects, and international collaborations, we equip students with the skills and knowledge to address critical health challenges in Zimbabwe and beyond.

Training & Workshops

We organize annual training sessions to equip students with skills in health education, leadership, and peer-to-peer facilitation.

Global Collaboration

About Us

The Zimbabwe Medical Students Association (ZIMSA) is a dynamic organization dedicated to empowering medical students across Zimbabwe. Through education, community service, and advocacy, we aim to address critical health challenges and shape the future of healthcare in our nation.

  • Community Health Initiatives

    We lead impactful projects like the IMUNZI Project, focusing on HIV/AIDS awareness and mental health support for youth.

  • Training & Capacity Building

    ZIMSA organizes workshops and training sessions to equip students with skills in health education, leadership, and peer facilitation.

  • Global Collaboration

    We partner with international organizations like IFMSA to promote global health initiatives and share best practices.
